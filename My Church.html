About Us

Friends church Huruma is located off Uganda road, 3km from Eldoret town at Baringo Estate-Kapkoros
It is the headquarters of Eldoret Quarterly Meeting of Soy Yearly Meeting. It also played a key role
in the formation of Soy Yearly Meeting of Friends Quakers.
